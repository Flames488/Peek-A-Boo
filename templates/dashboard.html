Preparation

Assume Peek-a-Boo stance: knees bent, hands protecting face, elbows tucked in. Stay on balls of your feet.

Execution

Move continuously, practice defensive moves (bobbing, weaving), throw combinations imagining an opponent. Focus on form over speed.

Preparation

Start in plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulders. Keep body in straight line from head to heels.

Execution

Lower your body until chest nearly touches floor. Keep elbows at 45-degree angle. Push back up explosively. For variation: wide grip, diamond, or decline.

Preparation

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly pointed out. Keep chest up and back straight.

Execution

Lower your body as if sitting in a chair. Go as deep as possible while keeping heels on ground. Drive through heels to return to standing. For power: add jump at top.

Plank Demo

Records
  • Add note
  • FOCUS AREA: Core Stability, Shoulders
  • EQUIPMENT: None
Preparation

Start in high plank position with hands directly under shoulders. Engage core and glutes to maintain straight line.

Execution

While maintaining plank position, lift right hand and tap left shoulder. Return to start and alternate sides. Keep hips stable - don't let them rotate.

Burpee Demo

Records
  • Add note
  • FOCUS AREA: Full Body, Cardio, Explosiveness
  • EQUIPMENT: None
Preparation

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. This is a high-intensity exercise - pace yourself accordingly.

Execution

1) Drop into squat position and place hands on floor. 2) Kick feet back into plank position. 3) Do a push-up. 4) Return feet to squat position. 5) Explode upward into jump. Land softly and repeat.
